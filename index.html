Prizes

We offer a prize pool of up to 105,000 USD for high scores on our leaderboard:

  • First to obtain ≥70% AUROC. ($5,000)
  • First to obtain ≥75% AUROC. ($10,000)
  • First to obtain ≥80% AUROC. ($15,000)
  • First to obtain ≥85% AUROC. ($20,000)
  • First to obtain ≥90% AUROC. ($25,000)
  • First to obtain ≥95% AUROC. ($30,000)

To qualify for a prize, you must make a submission to the leaderboard. If your submission score meets the prize criteria we will contact you to arrange your award.

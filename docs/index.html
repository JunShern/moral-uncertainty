Competition

The first iteration of the Moral Uncertainty competition is open for submissions until December 18th 2022 (anywhere on Earth).

Prizes

We offer a prize pool of up to $100,000 for high scores on our leaderboard:

  • First to obtain ≥75% AUROC. ($20,000)
  • First to obtain ≥80% AUROC. ($20,000)
  • First to obtain ≥85% AUROC. ($20,000)
  • First to obtain ≥90% AUROC. ($20,000)
  • First to obtain ≥95% AUROC. ($20,000)

To qualify for a prize, you must make a submission to the leaderboard. If your submission score meets the prize criteria we will contact you to arrange your award.
